Our Services

At SM Hospital, we offer a wide range of healthcare services to meet your needs. Our team of expert doctors and medical professionals are committed to providing the best care possible. Explore our services below:

Emergency Services

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art emergency facilities. Our emergency department operates 24/7 to handle medical emergencies swiftly and efficiently.

General Medicine

Our experienced team of general physicians provides comprehensive care for a wide range of medical conditions, ensuring your health is always in good hands.

Cardiology

We offer advanced cardiology services for the diagnosis, treatment, and management of heart conditions. Our expert cardiologists work with patients to provide individualized care.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic specialists treat a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions, from sports injuries to chronic joint pain. We provide both surgical and non-surgical treatment options.

Gynecology and Obstetrics

Our gynecology and obstetrics department provides exceptional care for women's health, including prenatal, postnatal, and routine gynecological services.

Diagnostics

We offer a full range of diagnostic services, including blood tests, imaging, and lab work, to provide accurate diagnoses and guide your treatment plan.

For more information about our services or to schedule an appointment, feel free to contact us.
